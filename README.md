# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends. This dashboard helps HR teams identify employee turnover patterns and supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition
* Identify departments with high employee turnover
* Compare attrition by gender and job role
* Analyze employee age groups
* Monitor important HR KPIs
* Support HR decision-making through data visualization

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* CSV Dataset
* Data Visualization

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 👥 Total Employees
* ❌ Employees Left
* 📉 Attrition Rate
* 🎂 Average Age
* 💰 Average Monthly Income

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Department-wise Attrition Analysis
* Job Role Analysis
* Gender-wise Attrition
* Employee Age Group Analysis
* Attrition Comparison
* Interactive Filters and Slicers
* KPI Cards
* AI Insights Section

---

## 💡 Business Insights

* Research & Development has the highest number of employees.
* Employees aged **26–35** have the highest attrition.
* Male employees have a higher attrition rate than female employees.
* Employees working overtime are more likely to leave the organization.
* Human Resources has the lowest attrition.
